Know Huddle

Know Huddle App awards points for correctly forecasting plays for a NFL Football game. App received third place in competition

  • View CODE

Background

Sports Tech Hackathon sponsored by the Seattle Sounders, was looking to integrate technology with sports. The goal of hackathon was to create an app that would generate revenues, and increase sport viewership. This group decided to look at traditional fantasy leagues because they are passive during live games. Know Huddle allows users to predict plays in real time, increasing fantasy players' interests in broadcast games. While this app can be used for any sport, the team focused on the NFL.

Technology Used

  • MongoDB
  • Python
  • JavaScript
