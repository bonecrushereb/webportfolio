Know Huddle

Mobile App is a real time play predictor for live games, which would enable app owner to add revenue through adds, subscriptions, event sponsorships, and competitions through private leagues and pros. App received third place in competition.

  • Click HERE to view CODE

Background

Sports Tech Hackathon sponsored by the Seattle Sounders, was looking to integrate technology with sports. The goal of hackathon was to create an app that would generate revenues, and increase sport viewership. Traditional fantasy leagues become a passive activity during live games. Know Huddle allows users to predict plays in real time. While this can be used for any sport, the team focused on the NFL.
